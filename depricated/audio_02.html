Ghost Rhythm is a working title for a game that unfortunately never saw the light of day. If it had finished, it would've been a DDR clone at first blush, but expand into a rhythmic bullet-hell as levels progressed and the mechanics and difficulty ramped up.

As you may have guessed, I don't have access to the game's art folder anymore. I hope you enjoy my improvised rendition!

It was a real challenge to get the tone right, and multiple ideas were rejected beforethese versions were selected as two of the would-be level scores.
What made the music for this project unique was the blend of themes: highly rhythmic dance music meeting video game asthetics, ghosts and medeival fantasy.
